# Recursive AI Incident Review Framework (Crisis Snapshot)

This document provides a rapid structural reference
for incident review involving recursive self-modifying AI systems.

It is non-binding and does not alter regulatory frameworks.

---

## Immediate Structural Questions

In the event of a high-impact AI incident:

1. Was recursive modification coupled to autonomous or external execution?
2. Was independent human refusal authority preserved at the point of deployment?
3. Could deviation propagate irreversibly beyond containment boundaries?

---

## Structural Focus

This framework does not assess capability.
It clarifies responsibility boundaries.

It does not advocate acceleration or restriction of AI development.
It addresses structural responsibility only.
